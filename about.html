Our Mission

Our mission is to lessen the numbers of abandoned, animals on the streets.
It is also our mission to rescue those animals who experienced mistreatment in their life, by giving them a forever loving home.

Our Vission

We believe that in the near future, we can eradicate the numbers of homeless animals who lives in the street away from the abusers.
We are also looking forward to the person who will eventually become dedicated to help.

Our Goals

One of our Main goal, is to find a forever home for these animals that didn't experienced being loved.
It is also our goal to give these rescued animals an necessary and appropriate care.
